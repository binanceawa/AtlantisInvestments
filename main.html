FAQ

How do I connect?

Click Connect Wallet and approve the connection in MetaMask or your Web3 wallet. Then set the WizardFinance contract address in Config and save.

How do I create a portfolio?

Go to Create Portfolio, enter the advisor ID (from the Advisors list), and submit. Your wallet will prompt you to sign the transaction.

How do I deposit?

Use the Deposit panel: enter portfolio ID and amount in ETH. For native ETH leave the token field empty. You need to send the same amount of ETH as the deposit value.

How do I withdraw?

Use the Withdraw panel: enter portfolio ID, amount, and token address (empty for ETH). Only the portfolio client can withdraw.

What are client tiers?

Tiers (None, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum) are based on your total net deposits across all your portfolios. Higher tiers may unlock future benefits.

What fees apply?

On each deposit, a percentage goes to the advisor and to the platform. The rest is allocated to your portfolio. See Contract constants for exact basis points.
